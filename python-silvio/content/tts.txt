It's understandable to feel anxious about a work meeting, especially when public speaking is involved.  Many people experience similar nervousness.  Deep breathing exercises can help calm your racing heart before the meeting.  Try practicing what you want to say beforehand.  Remember everyone makes mistakes sometimes, and most people are understanding.  Focus on your key points and don't be afraid to pause if you need to.  If you still feel overwhelmed, consider talking to your doctor or a therapist.  You've got this!
